HolyWork Movement

Navigating Faith & Work Together

Co-founded with my wife, the HolyWork Movement is a prayer apostolate dedicated to supporting Catholics navigating the challenges of unemployment, career transitions, and discerning God's call in their professional lives.

We believe that work is a sacred calling, and no one should face career challenges alone. Through prayer, community, and practical support, we help people discover how God is present in every aspect of their work life.
